Three simple questions about your proportions guide us to your shape.
For the best result
Stand straight, full body
A full-length photo from the front works best. Arms slightly away from your body, feet hip-width apart — as if you're standing in a shop changing room.
Wear fitted clothing
Loose or oversized pieces hide your natural shape. A fitted top and trousers, or a bodycon dress, lets us read your proportions accurately.
Good, even lighting
Natural daylight or indoor lighting that falls evenly across you. Avoid strong backlighting or harsh shadows — they can obscure your outline.
Plain background if possible
A wall, door, or plain curtain behind you makes it easier to read your silhouette clearly. It doesn't need to be perfect — just uncluttered.
Hair up helps too
If your hair is long, tying it up exposes your shoulder line — one of the key measurements we look at. Not essential, but it improves accuracy.

Five gentle questions about your natural colouring guide us to your palette.
For the most accurate colour read
Show your bare face
A makeup-free photo gives us your true skin tone and undertones. If you prefer to wear makeup, keep it minimal — heavy foundation can shift the colour reading.
Natural daylight is best
Step near a window with natural light falling on your face. Avoid artificial lighting — warm bulbs skew amber, cool LEDs skew blue, and both will throw off the reading.
Face the light directly
Turn to face the light source rather than having it behind or to the side of you. Even light across your face lets us see your skin, hair, and eyes without shadow.
Wear a white or grey top
A neutral top removes any colour cast from clothing that might reflect onto your skin. White or light grey is ideal — it gives us the cleanest read of your natural colouring.
Hair down and natural
Let your hair fall naturally so we can see its true colour and depth. If your hair is coloured, that's fine — just know we'll be reading both your natural and current tones together.

Our story

Dressed for you.
Every time.

Prêt-à-Fit was born from a feeling most of us know well — standing in front of a wardrobe full of clothes and feeling like you have nothing to wear. Not because you don't own beautiful things, but because finding the right thing, for the right moment, that actually works for your body, can feel like searching for something that was never quite meant for you.

The person behind this app works in finance. She is someone who cares deeply about being properly and elegantly dressed — not for vanity, but because the right outfit changes how you carry yourself. It changes how you walk into a room. It changes what you're able to do when you get there.

She built Prêt-à-Fit because she loved fashion, loved the ritual of discovering something new, but found the process exhausting. Scrolling through hundreds of items that weren't made for her body. Guessing at what would actually flatter her silhouette. Never quite finding that perfect piece — chic, elegant, considered — without spending hours looking for it.

Style is not one size fits all.
It never was.
Prêt-à-Fit exists to change that.

We use artificial intelligence not to follow trends, but to understand you — your shape, your proportions, your aesthetic, your life. Every recommendation begins with your body, not a bestseller list. We search live, real-time listings so that what you see is actually available, in your size, at your price, delivered to your door.

The result is a wardrobe edit that feels like it was curated by someone who knows you. Because, in a way, it was.

Style that fits. In every sense.

Privacy policy

Your privacy,
plainly stated.

We believe privacy policies should be readable. Here is ours, written in plain language.

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you use Prêt-à-Fit, you provide us with style preferences — your body type, sizing, location, occasion, colour preferences and style inspirations. This information is used solely to generate clothing recommendations for you. We also collect your email address if you create an account or contact us.

How we use it

Your preferences are passed to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate personalised recommendations. They are not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than producing your results. If you create an account, your profile preferences are saved so you don't need to re-enter them each visit.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your information with retailers or third-party advertisers. We do not use your information to build advertising profiles. We do not store payment information — this is handled entirely by Stripe, our payment provider.

Photo analysis (coming soon)

When we introduce optional photo-based body assessment, any image you upload will be sent directly to our AI provider for analysis and immediately discarded. Photos are never stored, never shared, and never used for any purpose beyond the single analysis you request.

Cookies

We use minimal, functional cookies only — to keep you logged in and remember your preferences between sessions. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, or if you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact us at support@pret-a-fit.com

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify registered users by email. The date at the top of this page always reflects when it was last updated.
